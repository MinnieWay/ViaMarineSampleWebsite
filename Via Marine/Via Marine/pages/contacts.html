Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our maritime services

What types of vessels does Via Marine operate?

Via Marine operates a diverse ships including oil tankers, chemical carriers, container ships, and RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) passenger vessels. Our company serves various cargo and passenger transportation needs throughout the Philippine archipelago.

How can I inquire about chartering a vessel?

For vessel charter inquiries, please contact our operations department directly at operations@viamarine.com or call +63 (2) 8765 4321. Our team will assist you with vessel availability, rates, and charter requirements.

What safety standards does Via Marine follow?

Via Marine strictly adheres to international maritime safety standards, including ISM Code, SOLAS, and MARPOL regulations. Our vessels are regularly audited and inspected to ensure compliance with all safety and environmental protection requirements.

What are your container shipping routes?

Our container shipping services in the Philippines. We also offer feeder services to national shipping lines for cargo bound for destinations outside the Philippines.
