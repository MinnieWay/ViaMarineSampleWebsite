VIA MARINE / TERBAN MARINE CORPORATION

Via Marine Corporation (VMC) and Terban Marine Corporation (TMC) specialize in the operation of tankers, barges, and tugboats transporting refined petroleum products. VMC was founded in 1985, and TMC was incorporated in 1994.

Beginning in 1978 as a unit of Vifel Fishing, VMC grew from a single small tanker to a full-fledged corporation. Over nearly 40 years, its partnerships with major oil companies have strengthened its commitment to strict safety policies. VMC ensures quality by operating only IACS-classed vessels and received its ISM certification in 1998.

Today, VMC employs around 200 seaborne personnel and 45 land-based staff. Its vessels are equipped with advanced cargo handling systems, modern communications, and pollution prevention equipment. The company aims to achieve a fully double-hull flee.

OUR MISSION

To be the premier provider of marine transport services in the petroleum industry.

OUR CORE PURPOSE

To be the first choice of charterers in terms of safety, environmental protection, and reliability.

OUR CORE VALUES
  • Efficient, safe, and reliable transport of petroleum products
  • ZERO accidents and ZERO oil spills
  • Reduction in permitted emissions
  • Maintain cargo integrity with no loss
  • Responsiveness to charterer needs
  • Loyalty to employees
  • Continuous self-improvement
